Welcome to Jamia Masjid Usman-e-Ghani

Your spiritual center in North Karachi, Sector 5-A/1. A place for prayer, community, and learning.

A Message from the Imam

"Assalamu Alaikum Wa Rahmatullahi Wa Barakatuh. We welcome you to our beloved Masjid. It is more than just a place of worship; it is the heart of our community. We strive to foster a welcoming environment for all, promoting the beautiful teachings of Islam through peace, compassion, and unity. Join us for our daily prayers, educational programs, and community events."

Our Mission

To serve the spiritual and educational needs of the Muslim community in North Karachi, providing a safe and welcoming space for worship, learning, and social gathering in accordance with the Quran and Sunnah.

Community Services

  • Daily five-time prayers
  • Weekly Jummah sermons
  • Quran classes for children and adults
  • Community Iftars during Ramadan
  • Nikah and funeral services
