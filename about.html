About Jamia Masjid Usman-e-Ghani

A cornerstone of faith and community.

Our History

Jamia Masjid Usman-e-Ghani was established in [1989] with the vision of creating a central place of worship and community for the growing Muslim population in North Karachi, Sector 5-A/1. Through the generous donations and tireless efforts of our local community members, a small prayer hall was transformed into the beautiful mosque we see today.

Named after the third Caliph of Islam, Usman ibn Affan (Ghani), known for his generosity and piety, our mosque aims to embody these noble qualities. We are committed to serving the needs of our congregation and the wider community, fostering an atmosphere of peace, learning, and spiritual growth.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of Islamic knowledge and a vibrant community hub that provides religious, educational, and social services. We aspire to nurture a strong Muslim identity in our youth and provide a source of guidance and support for all members of our community, inspired by the teachings of the Quran and the example of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).
